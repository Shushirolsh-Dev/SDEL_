Vendor Payment Terms

1. Platform Fee: A dynamic fee of 10% will be deducted from every completed order. This fee covers marketplace maintenance, support, and payment processing.

2. Fee Changes: The platform may adjust this percentage with 14 days notice. You will be notified of any changes before they take effect.

3. Payout Schedule: Payments are processed every Monday for all orders accepted before Friday 11:59 PM WAT. Minimum payout is ₦1,000.

4. Payment Method: All payouts are made via bank transfer to the account details provided above.

5. Reversals: In case of refunds or chargebacks, the platform fee is non-refundable and will be deducted from future payouts if necessary.

6. Tax Compliance: You are responsible for reporting your earnings and paying any applicable taxes.

7. Shop Deletion: Deleting your shop is permanent and irreversible. All products, orders, and earnings history will be removed. Any pending payouts will be forfeited.
