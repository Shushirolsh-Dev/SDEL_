About Sdel

Connecting the Global Campus – A letter from the founder.

The Mission

I’m Jonathan Peter, and I’m the founder of Sdel (thesdel.com). I built this platform because I believe that the university experience shouldn't be defined by technical friction or administrative delays. It should be defined by the exchange of ideas.

The Sdel Ecosystem

Secured messaging for fellow students with encryption for total privacy.

Enriched market to get all student needs at affordable rates with high delivery speed.

Explore page to get the latest happening and gist on the campus.

The Origin of the Layer

Sdel stands for the Students Digital Exchange Layer. I developed this myself—not because I wanted to go it alone, but because I experienced a very specific set of challenges that I knew I had to fix.

When I started university, I saw how much stress was caused by simple logistics. Delayed manuals and missing practical materials didn't just cause frustration; they hurt my grades. I realized that while the world had plenty of social networks for entertainment, it lacked a dedicated digital layer designed specifically for student productivity and academic survival.

Built for Utility, Not Distraction

Most social media is built to keep you scrolling. Sdel is built to help you finish.

I spent my time building this platform solo to ensure it stayed focused on one goal: helping students thrive. Collaboration is the default: Students can brainstorm and use fun polls to solve academic problems together. Stress is minimized: From day one to graduation, Sdel handles the logistics so you can handle the learning.

A Global Effort

Building this alone allowed me to stay true to the student experience. It’s a global platform—a focused, distraction-free space where university students from any campus around the world can access resources and stay connected.

If Sdel makes your journey a bit smoother, then the work was worth it. This is just the beginning for the Students Digital Exchange Layer.

Jonathan Peter

Founder & Solo Developer, Sdel
