DATA PRIVACY.

Global Privacy Protocol | Zero-Sale Policy

PROTOCOL 01

THE ANTI-BIG-TECH VOW

Sdel does not—and will never—sell your personal data to "The Suits" or 3rd party advertisers. Unlike major social networks, we don't profit from your identity. We profit by making your campus life 10x easier through sourcing and academics. You are the user, not the product.

PROTOCOL 02

WHAT WE CAPTURE

Identity

Username, Global Handle, and Email. This is to keep your Gists linked to you.

Campus Meta

Your University/Campus location. This helps our Runners find you for deliveries.

Gist History

The content of your posts and sourcing requests. This remains in the Sdel Universe until "Burned."

Security Logs

IP addresses and device IDs used during injection attempts. We use this to shield the campus from bad actors.

PROTOCOL 03

THE SECURITY VAULT

All Sdel data is stored in a secure, encrypted Supabase architecture. We use industry-standard Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure that no homie can access another homie's private data. When you delete a Gist, we trigger a "Hard Burn," purging it from the database permanently.

PROTOCOL 04

AI & IMAGE PRIVACY

When you upload a photo for the AI Sourcing Agent, that image is processed solely to identify the product you're looking for. We do not use your personal photos to train facial recognition or for any other biometric surveillance. Your face is your business; the product is ours.

PROTOCOL 05

THE NO-STALK RULE

We only use functional cookies to keep you logged in. We don't use tracking pixels to follow you around the web after you leave Sdel. Once you close the tab, we stop watching.

Privacy is a human right. Sdel is human-centric.
